The Thought-Starter: July 6, 2020

Is your algotithim biased? Build fairness into your machine learning.

Machine learning (ML) is transforming the marketing landscape, helping connect consumers to relevant content and products more accurately than ever before. And yet, while data and algorithms have profound power to do good, they can also do harm. By ensuring that the data fed into ML algorithms does not contain social biases, we can help make the output fair, inclusive, and beneficial to all consumers. Read on to understand the importance of ML fairness, so you can build an inclusive ML strategy.

" Bear in mind, there's only so much that the hottest algorithm can help you with if your data isn't very good.Put your effort into making sure that your data, your ingredients are relevant to the task. And if you have the most beautiful ingredients, you can get away with a very simple kitchen. "

- Cassie Kozyrkov, Chief Decision Scientist at Google Cloud
